Languages

I enjoy learning new languages and exploring how different cultures communicate. It’s something that naturally interests me, and I keep picking up more along the way. Currently, the languages I can speak are:

  1. English
  2. Hindi
  3. Marathi
  4. Spanish
  5. French

Art

I’ve been drawing since I was young , what began as small sketches slowly grew into something more constant. Art became a quiet way of understanding the world around me, a space where I could slow down and notice details that often go unnoticed. Over time, I found myself exploring different mediums, letting each one shape how I create.

I often work across:

  • Watercolour
  • Pencil Sketching
  • Acrylic Painting
  • Digital Art

Dance

Dance, for me, is a quiet release, a way to let go of structure and move freely. It’s less about perfection and more about feeling the rhythm, being present, and expressing without words.

Mostly:

  • Freestyle
  • Semi-Classical (Indian)
  • Hip-Hop
  • Lavani (Maharashtrian folk)
